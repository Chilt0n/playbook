P3: Add XXX Asset

Issue Explanation

Users reach out everyday asking if we can list an asset on our exchange, on CoinCap, or in KeepKey.

Follow these steps:

  1. P3 - T1: Confirm the asset (and additional information for CoinCap requests)
  2. P3 - T1A Asset (coin) requests may come in for ShapeShift, CoinCap, or KeepKey. If the person doesn’t specify which brand they want their coin added to, the best way to tell is by checking in Zendesk. Look in the top left corner of the ticket form. You should see a section that says “Brand”. It will specify which of the three brands the person is referring to.
  3. P3 - T2:Confirm the asset (and additional information for CoinCap requests)
  4. P3 - T2A: Confirm (does not require an additional back and forth) name of their coin.
  5. P3 - T2B: KeepKey asset requests are generally only fulfillable if the coin is added to ShapeShift, but we will never tell the customer that.
  6. P3 - T2C: If the Brand is CoinCap, you may need a little more information. The spreadsheet requires a few specific pieces of info regarding the coin. While you can find some of the information yourself, you may need to ask for:
    • A. The Coin/Token Name
    • B. The Coin/Token Abbreviation
    • C. Exchanges the Coin is Listed On
  7. P3 - T3: Use macro, based on product
  8. P3 - T3A: If the Brand is ShapeShift, you will send the “General > New Coin Request (SS)” macro.
  9. P3 - T3B: If the Brand is KeepKey, you will send the “Feature Request > Asset Not on Roadmap” macro.
  10. P3 - T3C: If the Brand is CoinCap, you will send the “CoinCap > New Coin Request” macro.
  11. P3 - T4: Edit and Send Macro
    • A. The macros mentioned in P3-T3 all have words like “COIN” in bold. You will want to replace this with the asset they listed. For example, if they want to add “WHATEVERCOIN” or “WEC” and we have no intention of adding that particular coin, you will swap out “COIN” for “WEC” or “WHATEVERCOIN”, depending on how they referred to it.
    • B. Once the macro is edited, you can send it.
  12. P3 - T5: Fill Out Asset Request (CoinCap Only)
    • A. If the coin request was for ShapeShift or KeepKey, you are finished after P3-T4. However, if the coin request was for CoinCap, you may need to fill out the columns in the CoinCap spreadsheet. Before you do, look for your coin in the spreadsheet using “Command/Ctrl + F” and typing its name.
    • B. If the coin is already in the spreadsheet, just add a number to the “# Requests” column.
    • C. If the coin is not in the spreadsheet, fill out every column except “Status”. “Other Info/Relevant Links” is not a mandatory column. If you have relevant links, you will add them there. If you don’t, that is okay.

Post-troubleshooting Steps

  • Step 1: Tag support ticket with CODE [ code = Issue # + Step you solved the problem with in the troubleshooting list, example: P1T2 (platform issue 1, troubleshooting step 2). If you can not solve with any of the above steps, tag as PXXNONE.
  • Step 2: If after hours, follow FIRES protocol
