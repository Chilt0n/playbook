P16: How to update firmware and bootloader (Platform with Updater App)

Issue Explanation

The updater app is used to update the firmware and bootloader on your KeepKey device. You will be prompted to update your firmware and bootloader during the initialization of a new KeepKey. Older KeepKeys that are already set up may still require updates.

Follow these steps:

  1. P16 - T1: Download the KeepKey Updater App by Visiting and select “Get the App”
  2. P16 - T2: Open the .dmg or .exe file after it downloads (make sure you do not have beta.shapeshift.com or the old KeepKey client open, or the Updater will not detect your device)
  3. P16 - T3: If “Update Required” Click "Update Now"
  4. P16 - T4: Choose whether you’re using a new KeepKey or if you have set it up before and follow the on-screen directions:
  5. P16 - T5: It will ask if you have your 12-word seed. Please make sure user has this before continuing.
  6. P16 - T6: Unplug the KeepKey and plug it back in while holding the button down.
  7. P16 - T7: Press the button on the device to confirm the update. You will see the arrow on the screen blackout once it's time to release. Do not unplug your KeepKey during this time.
  8. P16 - T8: Disconnect and reconnect your device.
  9. P16 - T9: You will see the screen that says “Bootloader Updated” - Success!
  10. P16 - T10: Move to next update (if needed), follow pop-up instructions as it walks you through the same steps. Once all updates are complete, you will see this view:
  11. P16 - T11: Close the KeepKey Updater and return to the Platform. When you return to the ShapeShift Platform, click "Connect a Wallet" and select "KeepKey". You’ll then click "Pair KeepKey" one more time to connect it.
  12. P16 - T11: If the updater app does not work, move to Upgrade Firmware with the KeepKey Client KK6 [link]
  13. P16 - T12: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note with details on issue.

Post-troubleshooting Steps
