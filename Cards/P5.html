P5: How to change your BTC account to SegWit

Issue Explanation

How to change your BTC account to SegWit

Follow these steps:

  1. P5 - T1: Log in to the ShapeShift Platform.
  2. P5 - T2: Pair your KeepKey with the Platform.
  3. P5 - T3: Click the Receive button at the top of the Dashboard page.
  4. P5 - T4: Select "Bitcoin" from the list
  5. P5 - T5: Copy your BTC SegWit address (starts with 3).
  6. P5 - T6: At the top of the receive modal, click "Send".
  7. P5 - T7:Select Bitcoin from the list and click the "Next" button.
  8. P5 - T8: Paste your BTC SegWit address in the "Recipient" field.
  9. P5 - T9: In the "Amount" field, enter the amount you want to send to your BTC SegWit Address. We recommend sending your full amount to get the maximum benefit of SegWit.
  10. P5 - T10:Click the "Continue" button.
  11. P5 - T11:On the next modal, click the "Send Bitcoin" button.
  12. P5 - T12:Follow the instructions on your KeepKey to confirm and sign the transaction.
  13. P5 - T13: When the transaction is confirmed on chain, all the BTC you sent will now be on your BTC SegWit address and ready to spend or HODL!
  14. P5 - T14: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note.
