P18: How Do I Forget My Device? What Does that Mean?

Issue Explanation

When you forget a device, you are un-Pairing it, or removing it from the platform’s memory. Your xpubs (balances and transaction history) will no longer be tracked r viewable. The private keys will not be wiped and all of your funds will still be on the hardware wallet. Any time you re-link the wallet, the assets will show up again.

Follow these steps:

  1. P18 - T1: Click on the ‘Settings’ in the menu on the left side of the page. You will see under ‘Linked Wallet’ that a device is listed and connected.
  2. P18 - T2: Click the blue button that says ‘Forget’.
  3. P18 - T3: A pop up will appear confirming that you want to forget the device.
  4. P18 - T4: Click the red button that says ‘Forget (Device Label)’ to confirm.
  5. P18 - T5: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note.
