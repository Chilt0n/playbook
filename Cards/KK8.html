KK8: Wipe (and recover) device from KeepKey client

Issue Explanation

A user may want to start fresh on a device and generate a new private key. In some rare cases wiping a device and restoring would solve functionality issues in the platform, but this step should only be used as last resort for functionality issues.

Follow these steps:

  1. KK8 - T1: Open the KeepKey Google Chrome App Client with device plugged in.
  2. KK8 - T2: Ensure the Platform is not also open - the KeepKey can only be detected by one program at a time.
  3. KK8 - T3: Click the Settings gear at the top left of the client
  4. KK8 - T4: Ensure you have your 12- word recovery phrase safely stored.
  5. KK8 - T5: Click ‘Wipe Device’
  6. KK8 - T6: Hold down the button on your KeepKey to confirm and wipe the device. Once this step is complete, your device has been wiped.
  7. KK8 - T7: Begin initialization process see P4 [link]
  8. KeepKey Google Chrome App Client (Recover):
  9. KK8 - T8: Open the KeepKey Google Chrome App Client
  10. KK8 - T9: Connect your device to the client.
  11. KK8 - T9: Connect your device to the client.
  12. KK8 - T10: Click Recover KeepKey at the bottom right
  13. KK8 - T11: Create a Label
  14. KK8 - T12: Set a pin
  15. KK8 - T13: Confirm your pin
  16. KK8 - T14: Enter in your 12-word seed entering the cypher letters correctly on your device. Cypher means that each letter is represented by another letter and the legend is on the KeepKey screen. This code scrambles after each letter is written so pay attention to the KeepKey screen for each letter because the cypher will change.
  17. KK8 - T14: Press ‘Enter’ & your KeepKey has been recovered!
  18. KK8 - T16: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note.
