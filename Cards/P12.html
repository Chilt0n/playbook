P12: How do I reset my PIN? (on KK client as of 8/19)

Issue Explanation

For any number of reasons, a user may wish to reset (change) their KeepKey’s PIN. If the user does not know their current PIN, they will not be able to reset it. There is one way to do this in the Chrome extension (old KeepKey client). This will eventually be moved into the platform settings in the future.

Follow these steps:

  1. P12 - T1: Open the Chrome extension/KeepKey client and click the settings symbol on the top left.
  2. P12 - T2: Click “Change PIN” from the Settings menu.
  3. P12 - T3: Use the cipher to enter the current PIN that is set on the device.
  4. P12 - T4: Use the cipher to choose a (new) PIN for your KeepKey.
  5. P12 - T5: Re-enter the new PIN to confirm the change.
  6. P12 - T6: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note with details on issue.
