P20: Connect Wallet (user already has seed and PIN)

Issue Explanation

User wants to connect a wallet to the Platform, and their wallet is not new, they already have the 12-word seed, and PIN for device. If setting up a NEW wallet, please view P4 [link].

Follow these steps:

  1. P20 - T1: Click the ‘Connect Wallet’ button in the middle of the page
  2. P20 - T2: Select the hardware wallet device you are connecting. Current Options: Trezor and KeepKey.
  3. P20 - T3: A pop-up window will appear on the top left side of the screen.
  4. P20 - T4: Click the device you’d like to connect
  5. P20 - T5: Screen will pop-up asking to enter your PIN
  6. P20 - T6: Enter your PIN
  7. P20 - T7: Click ‘Connect’ and your KeepKey will be connected.
  8. P20 - T8: If device still does not connect, move to P1-T2
  9. P20 - T9: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note with details on issue.

Post-troubleshooting Steps

  • Step 1: Tag support ticket with CODE [ code = Issue # + Step you solved the problem with in the troubleshooting list, example: P1T2 (platform issue 1, troubleshooting step 2). If you can not solve with any of the above steps, tag as PXXNONE.
  • Step 3: If after hours, follow FIRES protocol
