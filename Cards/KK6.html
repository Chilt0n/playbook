KK6: Upgrade firmware on KK client

Issue Explanation

User is unable to upgrade their firmware via the KK client if the the Updater App in the Platform is not working. Users can not downgrade their firmware.

Follow these steps:

  1. KK6 - T1: Open the KeepKey Google Chrome App Client.
  2. KK6 - T2: Plug your KeepKey in while holding down the button at the top of the device.
  3. KK6 - T3: Click ‘Upgrade’ on KeepKey device update screen.
  4. KK6 - T4: Confirm on your KeepKey that you have your backup sentence (12-word) by holding the button.
  5. KK6 - T5: KeepKey Client will say Device is Updating Firmware while the device screen will also say “Firmware update Mode”.
  6. KK6 - T6: Once it’s complete, Firmware will be updated.
  7. KK6 - T7: Unplug and Replug in the device. Firmware Upgrade Complete.
  8. KK6 - T8: If unable to assist customer with the above, escalate to: change Assignee > Escalations and leave internal note with details on issue.

Post-troubleshooting Steps

  • Step 1: Tag support ticket with CODE [ code = Issue # + Step you solved the problem with in the troubleshooting list, example: P1T2 (platform issue 1, troubleshooting step 2). If you can not solve with any of the above steps, tag as PXXNONE.
  • Step 3: If after hours, follow FIRES protocol
